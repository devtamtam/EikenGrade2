● 語数の目安は45語～55語です。

Extracurricular activities, such as sports and cultural clubs, are common in many schools. These activities offer students a chance to develop new skills, build friendships, and enjoy their interests outside of academics. Many students participate in these activities to enrich their school experience.

One of the key benefits of extracurricular activities is that they teach valuable life skills. Activities like sports foster teamwork and leadership, while cultural clubs help students develop creativity and communication skills. These experiences can enhance students' personal growth and build their confidence.

However, extracurricular activities can also have some downsides. The time and energy required can take away from students' academic work, leading to stress and poor grades. In some cases, students may feel overwhelmed by the pressure of balancing both schoolwork and extracurricular commitments.

解答欄：

解答例：

Extracurricular activities like sports and cultural clubs provide students with opportunities to develop skills and enjoy their interests outside of academics. They teach valuable life skills such as teamwork and leadership; however, they can take time away from studies, causing stress and potentially affecting academic performance.
