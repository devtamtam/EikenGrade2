● 以下の英文を読んで、その内容を英語で要約し、解答欄に記入しなさい。

● 語数の目安は45語～55語です。

The use of smartphones in classrooms has become a topic of debate in recent years. While some schools have strict policies against using phones during lessons, others are beginning to incorporate these devices into their teaching methods.

Some people argue that smartphones can enhance learning experiences. They can be used as research tools, allowing students quick access to information. Many educational apps are available, offering interactive ways to practice various subjects. Moreover, smartphones can help students develop digital skills that are essential in today's technology-driven world.

However, there are concerns about allowing smartphones in classrooms. Others worry that these devices may distract students from their lessons. There's also the risk of cheating during tests or exams. Additionally, not all students may have access to smartphones, potentially creating inequality in the classroom. Some teachers fear that overreliance on technology might hinder the development of critical thinking skills.

解答欄：

解答例：

The use of smartphones in classrooms is debated. Some people say they enhance learning by providing quick access to information and interactive educational apps, while also developing digital skills. However, others worry about distractions, cheating, inequality, and potential negative impacts on critical thinking skills.
